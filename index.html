Privacy Policy for Midnight System

Midnight System respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy outlines how Midnight System handles your information.

Data Collection and Usage

Midnight System does not store or retain any messages sent by users.

The bot only processes messages in real-time to perform its functionalities and does not keep any records of these interactions.

No personal data is collected, stored, or shared with third parties.

Security

While Midnight System does not store any messages or personal data, we ensure that all interactions are secure and follow Discord's guidelines and best practices.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be communicated via updates to this document.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Discord support server.

By using Midnight System, you agree to this privacy policy.
